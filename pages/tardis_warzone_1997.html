-- KEYS --

Tank Controls
key action
i or [up] move forward
k or [down] move backward
l or [right] rotate clockwise
j or [left] rotate anti-clockwise
[space bar] fire
Radar Controls
key action
w radar zoom in
q radar zoom out
Game Controls
key action
p pause/unpause
s toggle sound on/off
