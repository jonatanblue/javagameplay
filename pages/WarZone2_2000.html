-- CONTROLS --

Tank Controls
key action
[up] move forward
[down] move backward
 [right] rotate right
 [left] rotate left
[space bar] fire
 
 
Turret Controls
key action
t rotate turret right
e rotate turret left
r auto-center turret
 
 
Game Controls
key action
p pause/unpause
Esc Options Menu
 
 
Camera Controls
key action
F1 close range
F2 medium range
F3 long range
F4 ludicrous range
v
toggle internal/external camera
h
circling helicoptor view
